Account Deletion Information

This page provides information on how to delete your account data from GlintTec Apps.

Data Retention

We only retain your email ID, which was added while purchasing the coins.

Steps to Delete Your Data

  1. Open the app. If the app is deleted, please reinstall it.
  2. Navigate to the 'Settings' menu.
  3. Select 'Sign In/Out - Delete user info'.
  4. Upon successful processing, your email ID will be deleted from our servers.

Please note that deleting your data will remove your email ID permanently from our records.
