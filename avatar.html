Privacy Policy

  • Do we retain the Face Data?

    Our app does not retain any input face data. Once the analysis is complete, all input face data is immediately discarded.

  • Do we store the Result Data & for how much time?

    Generated data from the analysis, such as resulted image, may be temporarily stored on our servers for up to 1 hour to ensure the smooth functioning of the service. This data is automatically deleted after this time frame.

  • Is data shared with third parties?

    We use servers for processing face data. However, we do not share input face data with any third parties. The processed results are temporarily stored on our server for the aforementioned duration.

  • Does this application collect or use your personal information?

    This application uses an internet connection to analyze images with server services. Neither the app nor the server keeps your data.

  • This Application doesn't:

    1. Collect & use any personal information other than just analyzing & showing the immediate result. Image results may be temporarily stored on our servers for up to 1 hour to ensure the smooth functioning of the service.
    2. Store any personal data.
    3. Share it with third parties.
