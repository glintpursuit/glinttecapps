Privacy-Policy

  • Does this app collect or use personal information?

    No, If a user buys an in-app purchase (IAP), their email ID is used as a unique identifier to associate the purchase with their account, but it is only used for the purpose of restoring the purchase later on, such as when the user switches to a different device or reinstalls the app.

    This app performs image processing on the local device only. The internet is only used for in-app purchases (IAPs) and displaying ads. Images are not sent to the server.

  • This Application doesn't

    1. Collect & use any personal information other than just analysing & showing immediate result. (Without storing them)
    2. Store any personal data
    3. Share it to third party
