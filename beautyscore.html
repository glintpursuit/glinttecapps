Privacy Policy

  • Does this app collect or use personal information?

    No

    This app ensures your privacy by performing almost all image processing directly on your device. The only exception is the hair styling feature, which requires server processing to deliver the best results. Internet usage is limited to in-app purchases (IAPs) and displaying ads. Rest assured, your images stay local and are not uploaded to any server except for the hair styling feature.

    Hairstyle results may be temporarily stored on our servers for up to 1 hour to ensure the smooth functioning of the service.

  • This Application doesn't

    1. Collect & use any personal information other than just analyzing & showing the immediate result. (Without storing them)
    2. Store any personal data
    3. Share it with third parties
